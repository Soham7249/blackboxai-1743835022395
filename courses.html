Course Catalog

Explore our comprehensive curriculum designed to challenge and inspire

Advanced Mathematics

MATH 401 - Fall Semester

This course covers advanced topics in calculus, linear algebra, and differential equations with applications in physics and engineering.

Course Topics:

  • Multivariable Calculus
  • Linear Transformations
  • Partial Differential Equations
  • Numerical Methods

Prerequisites:

MATH 301 or equivalent

Physics Fundamentals

PHYS 301 - Spring Semester

A comprehensive study of classical mechanics, thermodynamics, and electromagnetism with laboratory components.

Course Topics:

  • Newtonian Mechanics
  • Thermodynamic Laws
  • Maxwell's Equations
  • Wave Phenomena

Prerequisites:

PHYS 201 and MATH 202

Computer Science Principles

CS 201 - Fall/Spring Semesters

Introduction to computational thinking, algorithms, and programming using Python with applications in data analysis.

Course Topics:

  • Algorithm Design
  • Data Structures
  • Object-Oriented Programming
  • Basic Data Analysis

Prerequisites:

None

Cognitive Science

COGS 350 - Spring Semester

Interdisciplinary exploration of the mind and intelligence, incorporating psychology, neuroscience, and artificial intelligence.

Course Topics:

  • Neural Networks
  • Language Processing
  • Decision Making
  • Consciousness Studies

Prerequisites:

PSYCH 101 or CS 201
